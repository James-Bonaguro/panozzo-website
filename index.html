A stronger homepage should feel familiar, capable, and easy to trust.

Families visiting a funeral home website are not looking for novelty. They want dependable information, a clear way to get help, and a presentation that reflects care and professionalism.

This direction keeps the site grounded in what is already working while improving the first impression, readability, structure, and polish. The goal is to make the business look more established online without making it feel unfamiliar in person.

What this redesign emphasizes

  • Bigger, darker type that is easier to read on desktop and mobile.
  • A layout that feels more premium without becoming flashy or unfamiliar.
  • More useful homepage pathways for services, obituaries, and planning.
  • A warmer, more confident first impression for in-person conversations.
