Why the homepage should feel different

The story should feel as strong as the reputation.

A funeral home like this is not selling novelty. It is offering steadiness, trust, and a sense that a family is being cared for by people who understand what this moment actually feels like.

The redesign leans into that reality. Instead of a dated utility-first layout, this concept frames Panozzo Bros. as warm, established, and personally involved — the kind of place a family feels comfortable calling right away.
