Our Heritage

Panozzo Bros. Funeral Home has been a cornerstone of the Chicago Heights community for over a century. Founded in 1926, we remain a family‑owned and operated business committed to providing compassionate care and personal attention to every family we serve. Our legacy is built on trust, tradition and the belief that every life deserves to be honored with dignity.
