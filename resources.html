Helpful Resources

We have compiled information to help you navigate the practical and emotional aspects of planning and healing. These resources offer guidance on benefits, grief support and frequently asked questions.

Veteran’s Benefits

If your loved one served in the U.S. Armed Forces, you may be entitled to burial benefits including a military funeral honours ceremony, burial flags and headstones. We work with local VA offices to ensure veterans receive the respect and recognition they deserve.

After Care

Healing continues after the service. Our After Care resources provide advice on handling legal and financial matters, connecting with support groups and coping with grief. We’re here to support you long after the ceremony ends.

FAQ

Have questions about funerals, cremation or planning? Our FAQ answers common questions to help you understand terminology, costs and options. If you don’t find what you need, please contact us directly.
