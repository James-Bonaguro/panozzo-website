Pre‑Need Planning

Planning ahead is one of the greatest gifts you can give your family. It allows you to make thoughtful decisions in a relaxed setting and ensures that your wishes are known. Our directors will meet with you to explore the many options available and create a program personalised to you and your family.

Why Plan Ahead?

Pre‑planning relieves your loved ones of having to make difficult decisions at an emotional time. It locks in today’s prices and guarantees your wishes are carried out exactly as you intend.

What to Expect

During a pre‑need consultation, we discuss your preferences, budget and any special requests. We document everything so your family is aware of your plans when the time comes.

Get Started

Contact us to schedule a no‑obligation meeting with one of our funeral directors. We are here to answer questions and guide you through every step.
